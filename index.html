Welcome!

The goal of this website is to bring together all the food assistance resources located in St. Joseph County. The latest information and requirements for all listed resources can be found here. Additional resources can be found on the MCHC website: mchc.nd.edu.

Included in this guide are:

  • Programs Requiring Enrollment: Federal or state-funded programs with enrollment processes.
  • Senior Resources: Programs for seniors, including nutrition and socialization.
  • Women and Children Resources: Programs for pregnant women, mothers, and children.
  • Immediate Food Assistance: Food pantries, soup kitchens, and gardens.
  • Other Resources: Additional support in St. Joseph County and neighboring areas.
  • Get Involved: Volunteer opportunities.
